01 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Encore VR Studios ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with Chill Bill, a virtual reality game available on Meta Quest (App Lab), Steam, and Itch.io. We are an independent developer and take your privacy seriously.

By installing or playing Chill Bill, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not install or use the application.

02 Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the game:

  • Username / display name — the in-game name you choose or that is provided by your platform (e.g. your Meta account username). Used to identify you in multiplayer sessions.
  • User ID — a unique identifier associated with your platform account, used to maintain your game progress and session state.
  • In-game activity — actions taken within the game (e.g. joining sessions, in-game interactions) for the purposes of moderation and anti-abuse systems.
  • Moderation data — if you submit a report against another user, or a report is made about you, we log the relevant interaction to investigate and act on it.
  • Technical / crash data — device type, operating system version, and error logs, collected automatically to help us diagnose bugs and improve stability.
VR Platform Notice Chill Bill runs on Meta Quest hardware. Meta independently collects platform-level data such as headset telemetry, controller input, and account information under their own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Meta's policy at meta.com/legal/privacy-policy.

03 Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

  • We do not collect real names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not collect payment information of any kind.
  • We do not collect precise location data or GPS coordinates.
  • We do not collect biometric data such as facial geometry or voice recordings.
  • We do not collect raw motion data (head/hand tracking) — this is processed locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not serve advertisements and do not share data with advertising networks.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party, ever.

04 How We Use Your Data

Data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate multiplayer sessions and display your username to other players in the same session.
  • To provide in-game moderation — including reviewing reports, issuing warnings, and enforcing bans where necessary to maintain a safe environment.
  • To diagnose technical issues, crashes, and bugs in order to improve the game.
  • To detect and prevent cheating, griefing, or other disruptive behaviour.

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving Chill Bill.

05 Third-Party Services

Chill Bill is distributed via the following third-party platforms. Each operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

  • Meta (Oculus) — platform services, account authentication, and headset operation. Meta Privacy Policy
  • Steam (Valve) — platform distribution and account services for PC players. Steam Privacy Policy
  • Itch.io — platform distribution. Itch.io Privacy Policy

We do not control the data practices of these platforms. Beyond these distribution and hardware partners, we do not share your data with any other third parties.

06 Data Retention

We retain in-game activity and moderation logs only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the game's moderation systems — typically no longer than 12 months from the date of the relevant activity, unless a moderation matter requires longer retention.

If you would like your data deleted, please contact us using the details in Section 10 and we will action your request promptly.

07 Children's Privacy

Chill Bill is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where applicable under local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Meta Quest devices and App Lab have their own age requirements. We rely on Meta's platform-level age verification for users accessing the game via that platform.

08 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data. UK and EU residents have rights under UK GDPR and the EU General Data Protection Regulation respectively, including:

  • The right to know what data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • The right to data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users via the game's store listing or our community channels. Continued use of Chill Bill after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
